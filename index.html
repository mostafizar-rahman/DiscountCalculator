How to calculate a discount?

A Quick Guide to Calculating Discounts:

  1. Determine the original price.
  2. Identify the discount percentage.
  3. Multiply original price by discount percentage.
  4. Subtract result from original price.
  5. The answer is your discounted price.

Example: Original price $100, 20% discount. $100 x 0.20 = $20 discount. $100 - $20 = $80 final price.

How do I calculate a 25% discount?

Calculating a 25% discount is simple: Multiply the original price by 0.25. Subtract the result from the original price to get the discounted amount. Enjoy savings effortlessly!
